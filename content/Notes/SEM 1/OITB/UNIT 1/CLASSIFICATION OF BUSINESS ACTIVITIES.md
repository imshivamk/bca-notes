## INDUSTRY
Making useful goods from raw material, the manufacturing and the production of goods 
1. Primary industry - 
	1. extract raw material from nature
	2. Extractive industry
	3. Genetic industry
2. Secondary industry - 
	1. make useful goods from raw materials
	2. materials are processed to produce goods for final consumption or for further processing
	3. FURTHER Classification:
		1. Manufacturing industries
		2. Construction industries
3. Territory industry - 
	1. providing services instead of goods as products in exchange for money
	2. This industry helps in the activities of the primary & secondary industry.
