## The Negative Impacts of Technology on Business:

### 1. Business dependence on Technology:

1. Dependency on technology is a huge concern. A variety of digital devices and apps have been developed for remote business operations and to conduct business.
2. We are using technology so often that we have changed our business habits and it is having negative effects on our business so badly.
3. Even a small glitch in technology can cut off the process of business. A very good example will be the use of the internet for online shopping.
4. If there is any issue in the webserver of the online store then it would be cut off from the customers and the business process will stop because of no alternate way of business.
5. In the modern world, technology is determining the ways for us to live. And we have shaped our lives according to it.
6. Examples of such dependency are credit cards, cryptocurrency, mobile transactions, and robots for production and processing.

### 2. Expanses on new technology:

1. Modern business relies on technology for multiple purposes. The dependency has reached a point that technology has to be upgraded to keep up with the competition.
2. Technology evolves continuously with time. Existing technology will outdated and needed upgrades result in a great expense.
3. There is not only an upgrade that will cost you money but also the maintenance.  
Usually, tech tools can not be sustained by local mechanics. The providing company or firms will provide maintenance service at a high cost.

### 3. Security risks due to technology:

1. <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">Businesses depend on the use of computers and digital devices for operations. The dependency on technology has exposed businesses to security risks</mark>.
2. The majority of business deals and transactions are made through computers and the use of the internet.
3. A<mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">ny carelessness from employees or workers can expose your business information to your competitors, hackers, or online frauds.</mark>
4. Your business could be just at a click apart from being theft or other cyber attacks
5. <mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">Hackers can steal valuable information, passwords, and information on bank </mark>accounts that can result in millions of losses. According to a recent survey, hackers in 2011 earned more than $10 billion.

### 4. Interruptions in the workplace due to technology:

1. Employees are using [technology to increase productivity](https://techbaji.com/technology/how-technology-increases-productivity-in-workplace/) and efficiency. But this technology is also driving their focus and attention away from work.
2. As <mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">we all surf continuously through the web and social media and we are addicted to using mobile phones for different purposes.</mark>
3. Employee <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">productivity is negatively affected</mark> by these interruptions and can reduce progress
On average, it is estimated that office workers can have more than 100 emails a day. That many emails can digest a good amount of work time.

### 5. Work overload due to technology:

1. The expanding competition has caused work overload on the employees as well as business owners.
2. The race of being on top has caused the business employees’ and owners’ health issues because they are <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">connected to the business or work on phones and laptops every time.</mark>
3. This bond of technology and work at any place anywhere<mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;"> has increased the overload of work.</mark>
