## WHAT IS MEMORANDUM OF ASSOCIATION ?

1. Memorandum of Association is<mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;"> the first essential documents</mark> in the formation of the Company.
2. contains the <mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;">fundamental rules regarding the Constitution and activities of Company</mark>.
3. It is the basic document which law down <mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">how the Company is constituted and what work it shall undertake.</mark> 
4. he <mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">purpose</mark> of MOA is to enable the members of the Company, its creditors and the public know what its powers are and what is the range of its activities .
5. The Memorandum contains rules regarding: 
	1. the <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">capital structure</mark>
	2. <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">liability of the members</mark>
	3. the <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">objects of the Company</mark>
	4. all other important matters relating to the Company.
6. It is the <mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">charter of the Company</mark>.
7. It <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">lays down the area of operation</mark> of the Company.
8. It also <mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;">regulates the external affairs of the Company in relation to the outsiders.</mark>

## PURPOSE OF MEMORNDUM

1. To <mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;">acquaint the prospective shareholders to know the field</mark> in which their money is going to 
2. what <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">risk they are undertaking</mark> in making investment.
3. Let the outsiders know certainly what the objects of the company.
4. <mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;">Whether the contractual relation</mark> into which they contemplate to enter with the Company is <mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;">within the objects of the Company.</mark>

## CONENTS OF MEMORANDUM OF ASSOCIARION

### 1. Name Clause:
1. The clause contains the<mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;"> name of the company</mark>.
2. The name selected <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">should not be identical with that of any existing</mark> company.
3. Also the name must not be one which is considered undesirable by the Central Government.
4. The name of the company should <mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;">end with the word ‘Limited</mark>’ if it this <mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">public company</mark>
5. If it is <mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">private company </mark>the name should end with the words <mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;">‘private limited</mark>’.
6. The purpose of adding the word ‘Limited’ is to enable all those who deal with the company to know that the liability of the members is limited.
### 2. Situation Clause:
1. In this clause, <mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;">the state in which the company’s registered office is located </mark>should be given.
2. <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">the exact address within the state is not given and only the name of the state is given</mark>, To <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">avoid any unnecessary legal formalities</mark> and expenses if there is a subsequent change in the address of the company.
### 3. Object Clause:
1. It should specify in <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">unambiguous</mark>/CLEAR language<mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;"> the objectives</mark> for which the company is formed.
2. Great care would be taken in drawing up this clause, as <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">the company will not be allowed to do any business which is not specifically mentioned here</mark>.
3. <mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">As it is difficult to alter the project clause later</mark>,
4. <mark style="background: #ABF7F7A6;">it is necessary that promoter’s should include in this clause, all possible types of business in which a company may engage in the future.</mark>
### 4. Liability Clause:
1. This clause states that the <mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">liability of members is limited to the face value of shares taken up by them.</mark> 
2. If a member has <mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;">already paid </mark>some amount on the shares, he can be <mark style="background: #ABF7F7A6;">called upon to pay only the unpaid amount of the shares.</mark>
### 5. Capital Clause:
includes:
1. particulars regarding the<mark style="background: #ABF7F7A6;"> amount of share capital with which the company is proposed to be registered</mark>
2. the <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">division for the capital into shares is fixed amount</mark> are included.
### 6. Association or subscription clause:
This contains a <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">declaration by the subscribers to the memorandum.</mark>
<mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">This declaration just precedes the names of the signatories to the memorandum.</mark>
