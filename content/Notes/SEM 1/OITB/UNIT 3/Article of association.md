## *Definition*

1. As per sec. 2(5) of the Act, Article of Association (AOA) are the regulations and by laws for governing the <mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;">internal affairs of the Company.</mark>
2. Its is called as the <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">secondary documents of the company</mark>.
3. They may be described as the <mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;">internal regulations</mark> of the Company<mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;"> governing its management</mark> and<mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;"> embodying the powers of the Directors and officers of the Company as well as the powers of the share holders</mark>.
4. They are framed with the object of <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">carrying out the aims and objects by MOA</mark>

*THE CONTENTS OF ARTICLE OF ASSOCIATION*

Article of Association contains the following particulars:

(a)
1. <mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">Division of the share capital of the Company</mark>
2. rules regarding <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">allotment</mark>, <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">issue</mark>, <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">transfer</mark>, <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">forfeiture</mark> <mark style="background: #CACFD9A6;">of shares</mark>
3. <mark style="background: #ADCCFFA6;">procedure for conversion of shares into stock and vice-versa.</mark>

(b) Procedure of <mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">holding and conducting the various meetings, notices, poll </mark>etc.

(c) <mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;">Voting rights</mark> of the members and rules regarding methods of voting.

(d) Matters relating to <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">appointment, powers, duties, qualification and remuneration of Directors.</mark>

(e) Methods of<mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;"> increase, reduce or alter the share capital.</mark>

(f) Rules relating to<mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;"> share certificates</mark> including duplicate certificates etc.

(g) <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">Declaration of Dividend and rules regarding its payment.</mark>

(h) <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">Constitution and composition of Audit Committee, Remuneration Committee.</mark>

(i) Terms of <mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;">appointment, remuneration, delegation of authority etc of Secretary, manager</mark> if any.

(j) Rules relating to
1. <mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;">account</mark>
2. <mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">audit</mark>
3. <mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">charging of depreciation</mark>
4. <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">creation of reserves</mark> etc.

(k) Rules regarding <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">borrowing powers</mark> of the Company and the <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">mode of exercise of those powers.</mark>

(l) Procedure of <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">winding up</mark> of the Company.
