### **List 1: NIMCET (Pure Mathematics Focus)**


1. **Set Theory** (Sets, Relations & Functions)
    
2. **Trigonometric Functions** (Identities, Equations, Solutions of Triangles)
    
3. **Inverse Trigonometric Functions**
    
4. **Quadratic Equations**
    
5. **Sequence and Series** (AP, GP, HP)
    
6. **Permutations and Combinations**
    
7. **Binomial Theorem**
    
8. **Matrices and Determinants**
    
9. **Coordinate Geometry** (Straight Lines, Circles, Parabola, Ellipse, Hyperbola)
    
10. **Calculus - Differential** (Limits, Continuity, Differentiability, Tangents & Normals, Maxima & Minima)
    
11. **Calculus - Integral** (Indefinite & Definite Integrals, Area under Curves)
    
12. **Vectors** (Dot & Cross Products)
    
13. **Statistics** (Mean, Median, Mode, Dispersion)
    
14. **Probability**
    

> **Note:** NIMCET excludes "Complex Numbers" (usually) and "Differential Equations" from the core syllabus, while heavily prioritizing **Vectors** and **Calculus**.

### **List 2: MAH MCA CET (Mixed Math & Aptitude)**


1. **Set Theory, Relations and Functions**
    
2. **Trigonometric Functions**
    
3. **Inverse Trigonometric Functions**
    
4. **Quadratic Equations**
    
5. **Sequence and Series** (AP, GP, HP)
    
6. **Permutations and Combinations**
    
7. **Binomial Theorem**
    
8. **Coordinate Geometry** (Straight Lines, Circles, Conic Sections)
    
9. **Differential Equations** (First order & Linear)
    
10. **Statistics** (Standard Deviation, Variance, _plus advanced topics: Correlation, Regression, Curve Fitting_)
    
11. **Probability** (Binomial, Poisson, Normal Distributions)
    
12. **Mensuration** (Areas & Volumes: Sphere, Cone, Cylinder) _[Class 10 Level]_
    
13. **Arithmetic** (Ratio, Proportions, Percentage, Time & Work) _[Class 10 Level]_
    

> **Note:** MAH MCA CET excludes "Vectors" and deep "Integral Calculus" but explicitly includes **Differential Equations**, **Mensuration**, and **Arithmetic** (Quantitative Aptitude) within the Maths section.
