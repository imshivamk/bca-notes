**Need of Accounting Software:**

1. What is a primary reason for the need of accounting software?
   a) To reduce the workload of accountants
   b) To automate financial transactions
   c) To improve accuracy in financial records
   d) All of the above
   **Answer: d) All of the above**

2. How does accounting software help businesses?
   a) By facilitating timely financial reporting
   b) By ensuring compliance with accounting standards
   c) By providing real-time access to financial data
   d) All of the above
   **Answer: d) All of the above**

3. Which of the following is NOT a benefit of using accounting software?
   a) Increased efficiency
   b) Improved decision-making
   c) Decreased financial transparency
   d) Enhanced data security
   **Answer: c) Decreased financial transparency**

4. Accounting software is particularly useful for:
   a) Small businesses
   b) Large corporations
   c) Non-profit organizations
   d) All types of businesses
   **Answer: d) All types of businesses**

5. The primary purpose of accounting software is to:
   a) Automate manual accounting processes
   b) Eliminate the need for professional accountants
   c) Increase the complexity of financial reporting
   d) Reduce the importance of financial data
   **Answer: a) Automate manual accounting processes**
