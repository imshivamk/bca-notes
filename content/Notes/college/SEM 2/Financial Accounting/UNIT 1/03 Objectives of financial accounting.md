## 1. OBJECTIVES OF FINACIAL ACCOUNTING

#### 1. Maintaining Systematic Records
- Maintaining <mark style="background: #3cbefc;">Systematic</mark> Records  of transactions for an organization in the accounting books
#### 2. Recording transactions
- <mark style="background: #3cbefc;">All transactions are recorded date-wise</mark> to be used in the future for further process
#### 3. Ascertaining profit or Loss
- Ascertaining profit or Loss whether the organization have eared  profit or suffered a loss
- <mark style="background: #3cbefc;">Income statement on trading a profit or loss is prepared for one financial year</mark>
#### 4. Ascertaining Financial Position
- by<mark style="background: #3cbefc;"> preparing balance sheets</mark>
- balance sheet contains <mark style="background: #3cbefc;">assets and liabilities that give information about the financial position </mark>of the organization
#### 5. Assisting The management
- provides financial information to <mark style="background: #9d64e2;">management for decision making</mark>
- Information includes the<mark style="background: #3cbefc;"> debtors and creditors, profit-loss etc</mark>
#### 6. Provide accounting info to interested users
- users analyze the info per their requirement
- users can be - 
	- <mark style="background: #3cbefc;">internal - management, employees </mark>
	- <mark style="background: #3cbefc;">external - creditors, tax authorities, investors</mark>
