Configuring the basic elements of a WordPress site, including the logo, site icon, and site name, can be done through the WordPress dashboard.
1. Install the elementor plugin and activate it.

#### 1. Log in to your WordPress Dashboard:
   - Access your WordPress admin panel by navigating to `http://yourdomain.com/wp-admin` and log in with your credentials.

#### 2. Change the Site Title and Tagline:
   - Go to `Settings > General`.
   - Update the "Site Title" with your desired name.
   - Enter a brief description or tagline for your site in the "Tagline" field.
   - Click the "Save Changes" button.

#### 3. Upload a Logo:
   - Go to `Appearance > Customize`.
   - Look for the "Site Identity" or a similar option (this might vary depending on your theme).
   - Here, you can upload your logo by clicking on the "Select Logo" or "Change Logo" button.
   - Choose the image file from your computer and upload it.
   - Adjust the logo settings if needed, and click "Publish" to save the changes.

#### 4. Add a Site Icon (Favicon):
   - The site icon, also known as a favicon, appears in the browser tab when users visit your site. It's a small image that represents your website.
   - In the WordPress Customizer, find the "Site Identity" section.
   - Look for the option to upload a site icon or favicon.
   - Upload a square image (usually 512x512 pixels) as your site icon.
   - Click "Publish" to save the changes.

#### 5. Verify Changes:
   - Visit your site in a new browser tab to see the changes.
   - The logo should appear in the header, and the site icon should display in the browser tab.
