1. Post is the <mark style="background: #2dba4e;">activity of writing or creating an article that will be published and seen by everyone</mark> who accesses the page of WordPress website
2. Post > All Posts

#### Formatting options of post :

1. bold , italics, strikethrough - select the text to format and click the respective option on the toolbar
2. Bulleted and numbered list - select text and click icon
3. Quotation mark - block quote feature. adds indentation and italicizes the text
4. Alignment options - left, center , right or justify

#### Other features on toolbar

1. insert "read more" tag - changes how much text the blog page shows
2. Distraction free mode - full screen writing mode 
3. show/hide kitchen sink - heading style, underline text, full alignment
4. paste 0
