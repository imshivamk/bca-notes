## What is data and information?
### Data:

1. **Data is the raw material that can be processed for any computing machine.**
2. For example − Employee name, Product name, Name of the student, Marks of the student, Mobile number, Image etc. - 
3. **Facts, statistics used for reference or analysis.**  Number, character, symbols, images etc., which can be processed by a computer. 
4. **Data must be interpreted, by a human or machine, to derive meaning.**
5. So data is meaningless unless processed.

### Information:
1. Information is the<mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;"> data that has been converted into more useful or intelligent form</mark>.
2. It is the <mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">processed, organized and structured data</mark>
3. For example: Report card sheet.
4. information is extracted from the data, by analyzing and interpreting pieces of data.ṇ
5. Information is meaningful
