1. Forests are the <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">dominant terrestrial ecosystem of Earth, and are distributed across the globe.</mark> 
2. Forests account for <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">75% of the gross primary productivity of the biosphere</mark>, and contains <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">80% of the Earth's plant biomass.</mark>
3. A forest constitutes many components that can be broadly divided into two categories that <mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;">are biotic (living) and abiotic (non-living) components. </mark>
4. Forest is made up of many layers such as f<mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">orest floor, understory, canopy, and emergent layer.
</mark>
5. Forests can be classified in various ways such as <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">Grasslands, Temperate, Tropical types with their numerous subtypes.</mark> 
6. Due to <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">increasing population and consequential expansion of civilization</mark>, there has been continuous depletion of natural forests over the centuries.


## USE OF FOREST RESOURCES

1. Forest is an<mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;"> important natural resource.</mark> Forests are vital for the <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">ecological balance</mark> and play an important role in <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">temperature regulation</mark> in the atmosphere.
2. Forests are natural and vast reservoir of <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">food</mark> and <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">shelter</mark> for animals. They provide natural <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">habitats for numerous species</mark> of plants, animals and micro-organisms.
3. Forests provide <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">timber, bamboo, canes, leaves, grass, oil, resins, gums, shellac, tanning materials, dyes, hides, fur, fruits, nuts, roots, tubers</mark> and other useful things for human beings
4. Forests provide<mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;"> raw materials for forest-based industries</mark>.
5. Forests are the natural home to <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">medicinal herbs and plants.</mark>
6. <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">directly or indirectly affects the climate</mark> (temperature, precipitation, moisture, underground water-table).
7. Forests <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">prevent floods and soil erosion, </mark>land degradation and <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">improve the quality of air and water</mark>.
