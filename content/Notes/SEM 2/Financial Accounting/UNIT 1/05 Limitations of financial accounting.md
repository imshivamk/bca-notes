#### 1. Supplies Limited Information
1. Only financial transactions information is provided
2. Information is only about the financial transactions
#### 2. Personal judgement impacts financial figures

#### 3. NO COST CONTROLLING
1. <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">Cost controlling is not feasible because the total cost is only known at the end of the financial period.</mark>
2. very difficult to control cost during day to day business
#### 4. Difficulty in Price setting
1. <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">Only when all costs relating to the product have been incurred, its overall cost can be determined</mark>
2. In financial accounting it is not possible to know the product price in advanced
#### 5. Recording actual cost
1. Only<mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;"> real cost of the asset </mark>is recorded in financial accounting
2. <mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;">asset values are subject to chang</mark>e but <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">only their purchase costs are recorded</mark>
3. price changes are not recorded nor reflected in financial accounting
#### 6. Incomplete revelation of cost
1. In financial accounting,<mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;"> only activities and transactions which can be measured in monetary terms can be recorded.</mark>
2. It <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">cannot record many other aspects important for business organizations </mark>like - effective management, product demand, industrial relationship

#### 7. No proper division of cost
1. The<mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;"> costs are not divided into Direct or Indirect costs, variable or fixed cost</mark> etc.
2. this division of costs helps organizations to <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">control cost</mark>
#### 8. No proper system for performance evaluation
1. <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">No system to create norms and standards to evaluate how effectively a company uses its resources</mark> - labor, materials, other costs etc.
#### 9. Technical subject
1. Its a technical subject, as the average person cannot understand it
2. <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">The understanding of accounting and its practices is necessary</mark> to analyze financial data and make wise decisions
#### 10. Accounting principles cant be evaluated
1. Principles cant be accessed or evaluated
2. <mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;">The actual performance cant be compared to the planned amount</mark>
3. <mark style="background: #ADCCFFA6;">The budget and the result cant be compared.</mark>
