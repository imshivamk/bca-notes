## What is Financial Accounting

1. Financial accounting is a <mark style="background: #9d64e2;">specialized branch of accounting</mark> that focuses on the <mark style="background: 9d64e2;">preparation and presentation</mark> of financial information for <mark style="background: #9d64e2;">external users, such as investors, creditors,</mark> regulators, and the general public.
2. The primary purpose of financial accounting is to determine company's financial performance and position of the company
3. Process of 
	1. <mark style="background: #9d64e2;">Recording</mark>
	2. <mark style="background: #9d64e2;">Summarizing</mark>
	3. <mark style="background: #9d64e2;">Analyzing</mark>
	4. <mark style="background: #9d64e2;">Presenting</mark>
All financial transactions as financial statements

## Scope of Financial accounting

#### 1. Record Transactions
1. Record each and every financial transactions taking place in a  business
2. Maintains a clear and <mark style="background: #9d64e2;">systematic record  of all information</mark> , in the form of <mark style="background: #9d64e2;">Journal and various subsidiary books</mark>

#### 2. Classify and summarize info
1. The <mark style="background: #9d64e2;">Collected information is categorized according to its nature</mark>
2. Fin. accounting involves classifying and summarizing all the fin. information recorded.
#### 3. Financial statement
1. <mark style="background: #9d64e2;">Preparation of financial statements like cash flow statement like Cash flow statement, Balance sheet etc.</mark>
2. They show financial position of a business
#### 4. Interpret financial info
1. Fin. accounting<mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;"> interprets information from the financial statements and analyses conducted.</mark>
2. understands and explains fin info for<mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;"> easy understandings and decision making</mark>
#### 5. Communicate all outcomes
1. communicates all <mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;">outcomes and information to external shareholders</mark> through an accurate picture of fin accounting
#### 6. Determine and maintain financial position
1. <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">determine actual image of financial position</mark>
2. finance is lifeline of business and its management is imp


## 1. OBJECTIVES OF FINACIAL ACCOUNTING

#### 1. Maintaining Systematic Records
- Maintaining <mark style="background: #9d64e2;">Systematic</mark> Records  of transactions for an organization in the accounting books
#### 2. Recording transactions
- <mark style="background: #9d64e2;">All transactions are recorded date-wise</mark> to be used in the future for further process
#### 3. Ascertaining profit or Loss
- Ascertaining profit or Loss whether the organization have eared  profit or suffered a loss
- <mark style="background: #9d64e2;">Income statement on trading a profit or loss is prepared for one financial year</mark>
#### 4. Ascertaining Financial Position
- by<mark style="background: #9d64e2;"> preparing balance sheets</mark>
- balance sheet contains <mark style="background: #9d64e2;">assets and liabilities that give information about the financial position </mark>of the organization
#### 5. Assisting The management
- provides financial information to <mark style="background: #9d64e2;">management for decision making</mark>
- Information includes the<mark style="background: #9d64e2;"> debtors and creditors, profit-loss etc</mark>
#### 6. Provide accounting info to interested users
- users analyze the info per their requirement
- users can be - 
	- <mark style="background: #9d64e2;">internal - management, employees </mark>
	- <mark style="background: #9d64e2;">external - creditors, tax authorities, investors</mark>
